History

The AlphaBrate Team was originally established in 2018
under the name XchuangC. Initially, our goal was to form a company.
After a series of discussions that spanned several years,
the AlphaBrate Team was officially founded in 2022.

Idea

Since the inception of our team, we have remained steadfast
in our original vision and idea:
to provide user-friendly, free, and open-source services.
We firmly believe that everyone should
have access to the best possible resources.
